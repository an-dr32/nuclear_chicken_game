Nuclear Chick-EN

How to Play

  • Press WASD to move
  • Shoot with the Spacebar
    or Right-Click Mouse
  • Press CTRL or B to dash
  • Press P to switch the music or M to mute!
  • Pick up health packs
  • Collect ammo crates
  • Destroy the mutant sheep!
